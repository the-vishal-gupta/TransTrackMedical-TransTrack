# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for everyone involved with TransTrack.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**
- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Unprofessional conduct

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces including GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to: NicMGildehaus83@outlook.com

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
